## What NOT to Do

- Do NOT omit attribution. CC licenses require it even for non-commercial use. On YouTube commercial channels the risk multiplies.
- Do NOT paraphrase the Japan Govt attribution. The exact text is required by the National Land Image Information license.
- Do NOT use CC BY-SA images without confirming legal review first. "Commercial use allowed" in CC BY-SA does not mean "free to use commercially without SA obligation."
- Do NOT add images not in this plan. Any new real image added to the video must go through the same candidate/license review process.
